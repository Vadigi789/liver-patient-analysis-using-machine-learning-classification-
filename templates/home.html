Introduction

Liver diseases averts the normal function of the liver.


Mainly due to the large amount of alcohol consumption liver disease arises.


Early prediction of liver disease using classification algorithms is an efficacious task that can help the doctors to diagnose the disease within a short duration of time.


Discovering the existence of liver disease at an early stage is a complex task for the doctors.


Themain aim of this project is to predict the liver disease using classification algorithms.
